The General Partners

Karteria Capital operates on a distributed conviction framework. The master fund's capital is divided equally across three distinct execution "Pods."

This structure ensures deep diversification. Rather than enforcing a singular monolithic strategy, each General Partner has total autonomy to deploy their portion of the capital using their unique methodologies, united solely by the firm's overarching risk-management parameters.

Tymon Cui

General Partner

Tymon oversees a quantitative framework deeply rooted in statistical arbitrage and mean-reversion. His pod relies on identifying transient pricing inefficiencies across highly correlated equities.

By utilizing high-frequency data modeling and sentiment analysis, Tymon's strategy captures micro-alpha while maintaining a strictly delta-neutral portfolio. The primary objective of Pod 01 is robust capital preservation coupled with steady, uncorrelated compounding.

Favorite Stock
NVDA Nvidia Corporation

Lincoln Cheng

General Partner

Lincoln’s allocation focuses on Systematic Macro and Event-Driven opportunities. His methodology bridges the gap between macroeconomic data releases and granular price-action modeling.

Pod 02 aggressively deploys capital during periods of anticipated market dislocation, capturing asymmetric risk-reward setups around earnings cycles, central bank policy shifts, and structural market events. Lincoln's approach is designed to inject convex growth into the broader fund.

Favorite Stock
PLTR Palantir Technologies

Nathan

General Partner

Nathan operates the Volatility Arbitrage and Options Pod. By trading implied vs. realized volatility, his strategy is explicitly designed to remain entirely agnostic to underlying market direction.

Using advanced derivatives structuring, Pod 03 generates premium yield during periods of market complacency and acts as the fund's internal hedging mechanism during severe drawdowns, ensuring Karteria’s returns remain smooth regardless of the S&P 500's trajectory.
