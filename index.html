About Us

We are fundamentally changing the dynamic between capital allocators and fund managers. Karteria Capital was built to dismantle the rigid silos of traditional hedge funds by establishing a distributed conviction framework backed by radical transparency.

Karteria utilizes a centralized capital pool that is divided evenly among our three General Partners (GPs). Instead of enforcing a single, inflexible mandate across the board, we empower our managers to be purely opportunistic in their areas of deep expertise.

Each GP deploys their specific allocation using specialized methodologies—ranging from systematic macro events to statistical arbitrage. This structure ensures internal diversification, allowing the fund to capture immense value across different setups while remaining tightly tethered by strict, overarching risk-management parameters.

How We Operate

Distributed Conviction

We utilize a centralized capital pool that is strictly divided among our three General Partners. Instead of enforcing a monolithic top-down mandate, we empower our managers to be purely opportunistic within their distinct areas of expertise.

Each GP deploys their allocation using specialized methodologies—ranging from systematic macro to statistical volatility arbitrage. This structure ensures deep internal diversification and dynamic alpha generation, tethered safely by firm-wide risk parameters.

Interactive LP Partnership

We vehemently reject the concept of the passive investor. At Karteria, Limited Partners are not just a source of capital; they are invited directly into the war room with an open line to our management team.

LPs are actively encouraged to attend strategy meetings and present their own investment theses to the board. The strongest, most researched ideas are sourced directly into our central stock pool for our GPs to evaluate, model, and potentially deploy capital into.
